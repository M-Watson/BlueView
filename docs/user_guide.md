# BlueView: User Guide

Welcome to BlueView! This tool is designed to help you catch up on missed content without the stress of infinite scrolling.

## Getting Started

1.  **Login:** Enter your BlueSky handle (e.g., `yourname.bsky.social`) and an **App Password**. 
    *   *Note: Do not use your main password. Create an App Password in your BlueSky settings.*
2.  **Set Your Range:** Choose a "Since" date and an optional "Until" date. The app will fetch all posts from your timeline within that window.
3.  **Fetch:** Click "Fetch Catch-up" to start loading your posts.

## Key Features

### 1. Author Grouping & Search
The left sidebar lists every author who posted in your selected time range. 
*   **Filter:** Click an author's name to see *only* their posts.
*   **Search:** Use the search bar in the sidebar to find a specific journalist or friend quickly.

### 2. Layouts & Grids
Customize how you read your feed:
*   **List View:** A standard vertical feed.
*   **Grid View:** See posts in columns (1-6). Great for visual catching up.
*   **Grouping:** Group posts by **Day** or **Author** to add structure to your reading.

### 3. Collections & Saved Searches
Save time by creating custom groups:
*   Click **Manage Collections & Searches**.
*   **Collections:** Group handles together (e.g., "News" with NYT, CNN, etc.).
*   **Tags:** Save frequently searched hashtags.
*   Use the "Quick Catch-up" buttons at the top to run these instantly.

### 4. Media Management
*   Toggle **Show Media** on or off to save bandwidth or focus strictly on text.
*   Click any post to open the original conversation on BlueSky.
