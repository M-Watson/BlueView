Welcome to BlueView

BlueView is an alternative interface for BlueSky that helps you read chronologically and intentionally, without the endless scroll.

  • Time-Bounded: Pick a start and end date to see exactly what you missed.
  • Organized: Group posts by Author or Day.
  • Visual: Read in flexible grid layouts (1 to 6 columns).
  • Focused: Create custom collections of your favorite journalists or friends.
